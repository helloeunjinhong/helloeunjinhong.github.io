### Description
**P.U.P.P.I.T** is a 3D first person shooting game implemented with Unreal Engine 4, which the player, who is a 'puppit' fight through the nightmare source to protect the child who's sleeping.

* **Role** :  Combat Designer

* **Team Size** : 6 programmers, 4 designers
* **Development Period** : September 2021 - April 2022
